🖤 The Dream Claims You 🖤

You take the map from her hands. It feels warm… comforting… safe.

“Just follow me,” she whispers softly.

The forest grows darker with every step. The stars above blink out one by one.

In the reflection of a still black pond, you finally see her true form — not a girl, but the Witch of the Dream, her smile stretching too wide to be human.

The map dissolves into smoke. The path behind you disappears. The animals’ voices fade into silence.

Somewhere far away, in the real world, your alarm clock rings. But you do not hear it.

Morning comes. Your body remains asleep.

You are no longer dreaming.

You belong to the dream now.
