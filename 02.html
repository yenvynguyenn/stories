🖤 The Witch Revealed 🖤

You follow her deeper into the forest. The trees twist. The sky darkens.

The map glows faintly in your hands — then begins to burn away like ash.

In the reflection of a black pond, her smile stretches too wide. Her eyes are hollow.

The forest seals behind you. The animals cry out — but you cannot hear them anymore.

You are trapped in endless sleep.
